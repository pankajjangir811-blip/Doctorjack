# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

Participants should be respectful, constructive, and professional. Technical disagreement is welcome; harassment, threats, discrimination, and publication of private information are not.

## Security and privacy

Do not post credentials, private target information, confidential scan output, or data collected without authorization.

## Enforcement

Repository maintainers may edit, remove, or reject content and contributions that violate these standards or the project's authorized-use purpose.
